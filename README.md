### 🚀 Architecting Discovery Platforms & Data Refinement

I am a **Discovery Platform Architect** and **Data Synthesis Designer**, and the founder of Quantome SAS in Paris. I specialize in data refinement: the meticulous process of preserving scientific correctness while transforming massive, abstract biological data into structured, interoperable platforms that can be run, stored, and scaled.

Whether for advanced biotech research or business applications, I build complex ecosystems that organize data in one place—portable, labeled, and instantly accessible.

What I'm Building:

* **100+ Go Applications:** Through Quantome SAS, I have authored and actively maintain over 100 Go command-line data-processing applications in proprietary repositories.
* **The Future of Agentic Engineering:** I am currently orchestrating a dense array of AI technologies to build autonomous agentic systems capable of writing and deploying complete Go Interlace applications.

While my current focus is Go and AI-based architecture, my foundation spans C, C++, Python for machine learning, Java, Pascal, and Fortran.
